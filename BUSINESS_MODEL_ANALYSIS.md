## ⚠️ Critical Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Market Saturation
**Reality:** 20+ competitors, some free (Zillow 3D Home)

**Mitigation:**
- **Niche down** — focus on luxury real estate, hotels, or restaurants
- **Differentiate** — emphasize your unique features (Rust backend speed, ReScript reliability)
- **Bundle services** — offer photography + tour + floor plan + teaser video

### Risk 2: Customer Acquisition
**Reality:** Real estate agents are bombarded with sales pitches daily

**Mitigation:**
- **Referrals** — offer $100 referral bonus to happy clients
- **Partnerships** — team with real estate photographers (they already have clients)
- **Content marketing** — write blog posts about "Virtual Tour ROI" for agents

### Risk 3: Technical Debt
**Reality:** Your app is A- grade, but not enterprise-ready

**Mitigation:**
- **Don't over-engineer** — service business doesn't need 99.99% uptime
- **Invest profits** — use service revenue to improve SaaS infrastructure
- **Hire help** — contractors for support, marketing, photography

### Risk 4: Burnout
**Reality:** Freelance + business building = 60-80 hour weeks

**Mitigation:**
- **Set boundaries** — cap service clients at 10/week
- **Outsource early** — hire virtual assistant for scheduling ($5/hour)
- **Take breaks** — one day off per week minimum

## 📞 Questions to Ask Yourself

Before deciding, answer these honestly:

1. **Do I want to be a business owner or a freelancer?**
   - Freelancer = trade time for money (service model)
   - Business owner = build systems, hire team (SaaS/license model)

2. **How much risk can I tolerate?**
   - Low risk = service business (get paid per project)
   - High risk = SaaS (lose money Year 1, potential upside Year 3+)

3. **Do I enjoy sales and marketing?**
   - If no → service business or license sales (less ongoing marketing)
   - If yes → SaaS (constant marketing required)

4. **What's my financial runway?**
   - <6 months expenses → service business only
   - 6-12 months → hybrid approach
   - 12+ months → can consider SaaS investment
